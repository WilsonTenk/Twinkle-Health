About Twinkle Health Foundation

Dedicated to transforming healthcare accessibility and empowering communities through innovative programs and sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality healthcare services to underserved communities while empowering individuals through education, support, and sustainable development programs. We believe that healthcare is a fundamental right that should be available to everyone, regardless of their socioeconomic status.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to quality healthcare and the opportunity to lead a healthy, productive life. We envision communities that are self-sufficient, educated about health and wellness, and equipped with the resources needed to thrive.

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Community Impact

Twinkle Health Foundation was founded in 2015 by a group of healthcare professionals and community advocates who witnessed firsthand the healthcare disparities in underserved areas. What started as a small initiative to provide basic medical services has grown into a comprehensive organization serving thousands of individuals across multiple communities.

Our journey began when our founders, Dr. Sarah Johnson and community leader Michael Chen, organized the first health education drama program in a rural village. The overwhelming response and positive impact on the community inspired them to expand their efforts and establish a formal organization dedicated to healthcare accessibility.
